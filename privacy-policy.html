Privacy Policy

Introduction

At NostalgiaCircle, we deeply value your memories and your privacy. Our mission is to preserve heartfelt stories from the past while ensuring that your personal information is protected with the highest respect and responsibility.

Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to maintain our platform and help you connect through shared experiences. This includes:

  • Your name and email address (when you submit stories, subscribe, or contact us)
  • Submitted content such as personal stories, images, audio, or videos (only if you choose to share)
  • Feedback or testimonials you provide voluntarily
  • Technical data like browser type, IP address, and device information (via cookies for site optimization)

How We Use Your Information

  • To feature and display your shared memories on our platform (with consent)
  • To respond to your questions, story submissions, or feedback
  • To notify you of featured stories, community news, or nostalgic newsletters
  • To improve our storytelling features and user experience
  • To personalize your journey on the site (like remembering your last read story)
  • To comply with legal, safety, or regulatory requirements

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze traffic. These small files help remember your preferences and understand what content resonates most with our community. You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings.

Content Submission & Public Display

Any stories, images, or other media you submit may be publicly displayed on our site to inspire others. We do not share your email address or personal contact information alongside any submissions unless you explicitly consent.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information:

  • With your written or digital consent
  • With trusted service providers who help us maintain the website (e.g., hosting)
  • To comply with laws, court orders, or government requests
  • To protect the rights, safety, or integrity of NostalgiaCircle and its users

Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Your Rights

As a user of NostalgiaCircle, you have the right to:

  • Access and view any personal data we have stored
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your information and submissions at any time
  • Withdraw consent from newsletters or public display
  • File a complaint if you believe your privacy rights were violated
